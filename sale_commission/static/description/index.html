Sale Commission Calculation

This module will help user to calculate the commission on a product in th sale order line based on the commission in the sale order.

Prerequisites before Installation

No prerequisites are required before the installation.

Configurations

No configurations needed.

New field in sale order for inputting the commission that will be applied on the order.

The commission that will be applied on the product in the sale order line which will be calculated based on the subtotal of the product

A smart button in the customer form view to view the all the sale order lines and the total of all lines.

View of all the order lines of the customer.
